MK Hatch Pilot Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 12, 2026

MK Hatch Pilot helps users manage egg incubation, chick care, and poultry marketplace activity. This policy explains what information the app uses and how that information supports the service.

Information We Use

  • Account details such as name, phone number, location, and optional MeekyCart email
  • Incubation records such as batches, daily logs, candling notes, and hatch results
  • Marketplace content such as listings, inquiry messages, seller verification details, and saved listings
  • Photos uploaded for marketplace listings
  • Technical service data required to operate Firebase Authentication, Firestore, Storage, and Hosting

How We Use Information

  • To provide hatch tracking, reminders, and batch history
  • To connect buyers and sellers in the marketplace
  • To review seller verification requests and reduce fraud risk
  • To connect hatch-product purchases from MeekyCart where users choose to link those details
  • To improve reliability, security, and product quality

Payments and Marketplace Safety

MK Hatch Pilot does not hold buyer funds. Transactions are arranged directly between buyer and seller using offline payment methods such as cash on delivery or other local payment arrangements chosen by the parties involved.

Third-Party Services

The app currently uses services such as:

  • Firebase Authentication
  • Cloud Firestore
  • Firebase Storage
  • Firebase Hosting
  • Google Sign-In

Data Retention

We keep user data for as long as it is needed to provide the service, maintain account history, support marketplace communication, and satisfy reasonable business or security requirements.

Contact

For privacy or account questions, contact the MK Hatch Pilot team using the support details you provide in your Play Store listing or official business channels.
