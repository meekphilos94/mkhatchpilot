# MK Hatch Pilot Store Listing Draft

## App Title

MK Hatch Pilot

## Short Description

Track hatches, manage chicks, and sell poultry in one app.

## Full Description

MK Hatch Pilot helps poultry farmers and hatch businesses manage the full journey from incubation to sale.

Use the app to:

- create and manage hatch batches
- log daily temperature, humidity, turning, and water checks
- track candling results and egg-by-egg findings
- record hatch outcomes and early brooding care
- create poultry marketplace listings
- chat with buyers and manage inquiries
- connect hatch product purchases from MeekyCart

MK Hatch Pilot is built for practical daily farm work. It helps both beginners and growing hatch businesses stay organized, reduce mistakes, and move chicks from incubator to market more confidently.

Marketplace note:

- payments are arranged directly between buyer and seller
- users are encouraged to meet safely, inspect stock, and avoid scam behavior
